Website:
Fidor Solution Bank

About the company

Fidor Solutions Bank is a leading provider of open digital banking services. It combines exceptional consulting expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver highly customer-focused digital banking solutions worldwide.

The assignement

As a member of the Marketing and Content team, we were tasked with creating a B2B website from scratch within a tight deadline. Working closely with a writer and the IT specialist, we developed an appealing and responsive website tailored to our B2B clients' needs.

My process

  1. Conduct meetings with stakeholders to understand their website requirements, including goals, target audience, budget, and deadline.
  2. Create a sitemap and select SEO-friendly URLs.
  3. Request the IT specialist to set up a local development environment of our CMS.
  4. Analyze the existing design and structure of the company theme. Customize the theme's appearance, including colors, typography, and branding elements.
  5. Create and implement the pages using the content provided by the writer, using the appropriate HTML syntax. Edit the CSS locally if needed.
  6. Create forms and perform testing with the commercial team.
  7. Select illustrative pictures, as well as create infographics.
  8. Ensure the website design is responsive and mobile-friendly. Test the website across various browsers and devices.
  9. Conduct thorough testing of the website's functionality, forms, links, and overall user experience.
  10. Optimize the website's on-page SEO elements, including meta tags, headings, and keywords.
  11. Present the completed website to the stakeholders for review. Gather their feedback and make necessary revisions or adjustments as per their requests.
  12. Buy the pictures, compress the images and test the page load speed.
  13. After the website goes live, ensure all links, forms, and functionalities are working correctly.
  14. Provide maintenance assistance, including updates and troubleshooting. Scan for dead links and implement redirection if necessary.
